Final Project

Clean water purification

create some kind of a bottle that can be made in different sizes, the bottom of the bottle will have an implemented purification system that purifies water .
the design will aid people that live in villages and have no access to drinkable water, each household or a family will have the product and all they need is a nearby river or any type of water and they can self-purify it to make it drinkable .
